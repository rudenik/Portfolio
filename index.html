About Me

I am a technology fixer. Much like the hollywood fixer, my name is mentioned with hushed tones in dark, smoke-filled rooms. Mr. Cain, called upon only for the most dire of situations. Yet, there is no problem too small ... even if the answer is simply “have you turned it off and on again?”. While trying to solve the most difficult of problems, I transform into a veritable Sam Spade or Nick Charles.
